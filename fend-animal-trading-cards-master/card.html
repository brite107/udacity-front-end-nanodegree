Labrador Retriever

The sweet, smart, and friendly Labrador Retriever is the most popular type of dog in America.

  • Personality: Active, Friendly, Outgoing
  • Energy Level: High
  • Trainability: Eager to Please
  • Average Size: 65-80 pounds (male), 55-70 pounds (female)

The labrador retriever has three standard colors: black, yellow, and chocolate. From year to year, they continue to be one of the most popular breeds for families due to their friendly personality and dependable disposition toward children. Because of their trainability and stable temperament, labradors are often used as service dogs for the disabled.
